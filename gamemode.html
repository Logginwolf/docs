Background

The Werewolves of Miller's Hollow has been released at early 2000s and it becomes peak during pandamic between March and May 2020 according to Google Trends1. It is because the game can be played without properties as well as increase usages of online conferencing software that it assists peoples to retains social interactions when going outside was forbidden. Depending on regions scope, it also reveals that East Asia has the most interests rates amongs with other geocultural1. Therefore, the derived version of Werewolf were invented to minimize complexity of player roles and eliminate third teams that these improvment can make the game progress becomes compact to attract peoples who have limited vacant times.
