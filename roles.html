Roles descriptions

Origin

Original game features eight roles from base game and five additional roles from New Moon expansion. Sheriff is also features as a honorable role for participants that it can only be obtained by voting at initial turn and be nominated if current sheriff is going to be eliminated, which player who honored must be accepted. During judging session, vote from player with sheriff title is counted as double and coexisted with original roles.

Base game
Role ID Name Required roles Primary party Max no. of players Description
1 Villager Yes Townfolk ∞

A fundamental role with no actions at night.
2 Seer Yes Townfolk 1

Allow revealing one of the participants' primary party at night via moderator.

The result must be exposed via gestures (e.g. Thumbs up and down indicates as good and bad person accordingly.)
3 Cupid No Third party

Select two participants (include Cupid itself) to becomes as a couple at the initial state. When one of the couple be elimated, another player in the couple also be eliminated.

Cupid's actual party is affected by combinations of couple's parties as below:

  • Townfolk - Townfolk (or Cupid): Townfolk
  • Werewolf - Werewolf: Werewolf
  • Werewolf - Townfolk (or Cupid): Third party
  • Piper with any roles also consider as third party role.

Win conditions in third party role: eliminate (for Werewolf teams up with non-Werewolf party except Piper) or charm (if Piper is in the couple) all participants except himself/herself and another member in couple, no matter which parties are them.

4 Witch Yes Townfolk

Allow heal, poison or do both to one of the participants at night. Each abilities can be used once per game round only.
5 Hunter Yes Townfolk

Unless eliminated by poisoning or couple being elimated, Hunter can pick one of the participants to strike back right after his devoution has been announced.

6 Thief No Third party

Two additional Villager card added into card deck and randomly reserve two role cards before distributing role card to participants. Thief can reveal the reserved cards and pick one of the card to exchange his/her role.
7 Little girl No Townfolk

She can secretly peek when Werewolfs are going to murder one of the members in Townfolk at night. However, she will be elimated if discovered by Werewolves.
8 Werewolves Yes Werewolf Variable

They can eliminate one of the non-Werewolf participant at night and disguise as Townfolk during the day.
New Moon
Role ID Name Required roles Primary party Max no. of players Description
9 Idiot No Townfolk 1

Idiot can continue to play with revoked voting ability if he/she was picked during judging session.
10 Elder No Townfolk

When he/she is murdered by Werewolves first time, all Townfolk party members' special power will be disapperared. He/She becomes eliminated if either murdered by Werewolves twice or causing deaths, which commited by other non-Werewolf party member.

If he/she revived by Witch, his/her life is counted as one.
11 Scapegoat No Townfolk

If vote result from judging session is tie, Scapegoat will be eilimated automatically instead of moderator's preference. Once eliminated in judging session, he can revokes voting abilities to serval participants on the next day judging session. Revoke voting abilities to all participants is forbidden.
12 Defender No Townfolk

Each night Defender can protect any participants (include himself), who not be protected last night to prevent being murdered by Werewolf.
13 Piper No Third party

Each nights, Piper can charms two participants until every participants are being charmed except him/her.

Although players who being charmed does not affect their abilities from the roles, it cannot be cured by any methods.

Win condition: All other participants are being charmed.
Chinese

The Chinese variant allows participants who playing majority Werewolf party and specified roles in Townfolk party to self-reveal his/her role during discussion. However, unless mentioned in role description, player who self-revealed will be eliminated automatically as well as entering night routine immediately.

Role ID Name Required roles Primary party Max no. of players Description
1 村民 (Villager) Yes Townfolk (Same preferences from original game)
2 預言家/预言家 (Seer) Yes Townfolk
4 女巫 (Witch) Yes Townfolk
5 獵人/猎人 (Hunter) Yes Townfolk
8 狼人 (Werewolves) Yes Werewolf
9 白癡 (Idiot) No Townfolk
12 守衛/守卫 (Defender) No Townfolk 1 The behaviour is the same from original game, but the protection is no longer be applied if the protected player is also healed by witch.
14 騎士/骑士 (Knight) No Townfolk

Knight can reveal himself/herself to make a duel with one of the participants during discussion session. If the chosen player is werewolf party, he/she will be eliminated and their special skills can no longer be activated (if applied); then, the game fall at night immediately. Otherwise, resume the discussion. Knight will be elimated automatically once he/she activate the duel.
15 狼王 (Werewolf king) No Werewolf 1, as well as comply the ratio between Townfolk and Werewolf parties

His behaviour is identical with hunter, which take down one of the participant when he is being eliminated. Hunter and Werewolf king are no longer allow to reveal their card if one of them are eliminated.
16 狼美人 (Wolfbeauty) No Werewolf

After she commited murder with other werewolf, she can seduce another player that once she eliminated next day, the victum will also be eliminated with no special ability can be activated. Otherwise, the seduction discarded and repeat the procedure at night.

Self-reveal is forbidden for this role.
