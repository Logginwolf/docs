Every roles who mentioned in this page is based on the manual from base game and New Moon expansion packs are retrived from manual from The Pact. Considering unpopularity of the Werewolf's expansion packs, roles from The Village and Characters expansion pack are not included due to overcomplexity of rules and ambiguous description accordingly.

Most variants inhert majority roles and abilities from the original game with localization. Therefore, their localized name will be appeared first along with translation or equivalent name from original game if applied.

Roles descriptions

Origin
Base game
Role ID Name Required roles Primary party Max no. of players Description
1 Villager Yes Townfolk ∞

A fundamental role with no actions at night.
2 Seer Yes Townfolk 1

Allow revealing one of the participants' primary party at night via moderator.
3 Cupid No Third party 1

Select two participants (include Cupid itself) to becomes as a couple at the initial state.

Cupid's actual party is affected by combinations of couple's parties as below:

  • Townfolk - Townfolk (or Cupid): Townfolk
  • Werewolf - Werewolf: Werewolf
  • Werewolf (or Piper) - Townfolk (or Cupid): Third party

Win conditions in third party role: eliminate all participants except himself/herself and the couple, no matter which parties are them.

4 Witch Yes Townfolk 1

Allow heal, poison or do both to one of the participants at night. Each abilities can be used once per game round only.
5 Hunter Yes Townfolk 1

Unless eliminated by poisoning or couple being elimated, Hunter can pick one of the participants to strike back right after his devoution has been announced.
6 Thief No Third party 1

Two additional Villager card added into card deck and randomly reserve two role cards before distributing role card to participants. Thief can reveal the reserved cards and pick one of the card to exchange his/her role.
7 Little girl No Townfolk 1

She can secretly peek when Werewolfs are going to murder one of the members in Townfolk at night. However, she will be elimated if discovered by Werewolves.
8 Werewolves No Werewolf Variable

They can elimilate one of the non-Werewolf participant at night and disguise as Townfolk during the day.
New Moon
Role ID Name Required roles Primary party Max no. of players Description
9 Idiot No Townfolk 1

Idiot can continue to play with revoked voting ability if he/she was picked during judging session.
10 Elder No Townfolk 1

When he/she is murdered by Werewolves first time, all Townfolk party members' special power will be disapperared. He/She becomes elimilated if either murdered by Werewolves twice or causing deaths, which commited by other non-Werewolf party member.

If he/she revived by Witch, his/her life is counted as one.
11 Scapegoat No Townfolk 1

If vote result from judging session is tie, Scapegoat will be eilimated automatically instead of moderator's preference. Once eliminated in judging session, he can revokes voting abilities to serval participants on the next day judging session. Revoke voting abilities to all participants is forbidden.
12 Defender No Townfolk 1

Each night Defender can protect any participants (include himself), who not be protected last night to prevent being murdered by Werewolf.
13 Piper No Third party 1

Each nights, Piper can charms two participants until every participants are being charmed except him/her.

Although players who being charmed does not affect their abilities from the roles, it cannot be cured by any methods.

Win condition: All other participants are being charmed.
