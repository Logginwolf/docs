This website aims to provide open data standard from Logginwolf. Currently, documentations for general usages of this application is unavailable.

Logginwolf is an application for recording progress of The Werewolves of Miller's Hollow and it's variants. Currently, Logginwolf supports archiving these variants as below:

  • Origin
    • Basic
    • Expansion pack 1: New Moon
    • Expansion pack 2: The Village
  • Chinese (狼人杀/狼人殺)

The program is adapted Flutter as frontend framework, which can be deployed to mobile (Android)1 and desktop (Windows, Linux)1 platforms at once. Although Logginwolf is not an open source program, dependencies derived from this project will keep open and release to pub.dev as well as publishing source codes into GitHub. All repositories will be published under MPL 2.0 license.

Background

The Werewolves of Miller's Hollow has been released at early 2000s as a theme of Mafia and it becomes peak during pandamic between March and May 2020 according to Google Trends2. It is because the game can be played without properties as well as increase usages of online conferencing software that it assists peoples to retains social interactions when going outside was forbidden. Depending on regions scope, it also reveals that East Asia has the most interests rates amongs with other geocultural2. Therefore, the derived version of Werewolf were invented to minimize complexity of player roles and eliminate third teams that these improvment can make the game progress becomes compact to attract peoples who have limited vacant times.

Rules

Logginwolf adapts partial rules from the official The Werewolves of Miller's Hollow manual:

  • Players (Including moderator): 6 - 48 peoples

  • Parties: Townfolk, Werewolf and third-party3

  • Win condition:

    • Werewolf: Elimilate all members in Townfolk party and prevent being identified during discussion section
    • Townfolk: Find all suspects of Werewolf before being eliminated
    • Third-party roles has unique winning condition, please refer to role page for more details

  • Supported players in Werewolf party4:

    Range of participants Minimum Maximum
    6 - 7 2 2
    8 - 11 4
    12 - 23 4 (1/3 of participants, floored if cannot divided to integer)
    24 - 47 6

Guidance when using Logginwolf

  • Logginwolf is an assist program for moderator, no participants are allowed to peek and control the program during in the game progress unless some instructions requires parsing to participants for some process.

  • The game progress are saved into device storage automatically at the end of initial, night and day turn. If proceeding undo after at the end of game turn, all previous turn records are discared. Therefore, please verify applying record before proceeding next turn.

  • Archive can only be generated when entire game progress is completed and afterward.
